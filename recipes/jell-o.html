Jell-O

Description

this easy cranberry Jell-O salad for Thanksgiving is the most popular dish we had! Cherry-flavored gelatin teams up with jellied cranberry sauce and crushed pineapple for a fruity flavor that can't be beat.

This cranberry Jell-O salad will add a bright and fruity burst of nostalgic color to your Thanksgiving table. According to many members of the Allrecipes community, even the pickiest eaters will request it year after year.

Ingredients

  • Water: Before you start combining the ingredients, you'll bring two cups of water to a boil.
  • Gelatin mix: You'll need two packages of cherry-flavored gelatin mix (such as Jell-O).
  • Cranberry sauce: Opt for canned, jellied cranberry sauce.
  • Pineapple: A can of crushed pineapple adds even more bright, fruity flavor to this cranberry Jell-O salad.
  • Nuts: The recipe calls for chopped pecans, but you can use walnuts if you prefer.

Steps

  2. Gather all ingredients.
  3. Combine boiling water and gelatin mix in a bowl; stir until gelatin is dissolved.
  4. Stir in cranberry sauce and crushed pineapple.
  5. Transfer to a large glass serving dish or 12 individual dishes. Sprinkle pecans over top.
  6. Chill until set, 3 to 4 hours for individual dishes, or 4 hours to overnight for one large serving dish.
